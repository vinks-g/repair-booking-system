Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions customers ask before booking a repair.

What devices can Vinton Solutions repair?

We repair phones, laptops, TVs, sound systems, selected appliances, CCTV systems, biometric systems, and other electronics support equipment.

What is the repair process?

You book the repair, we diagnose the issue, provide a repair estimate, request payment where needed, carry out the repair, and keep you updated until completion.

What does the repair estimate include?

The estimate may include labor, replacement parts, installation work, and any required deposit depending on the type of repair or service requested.

Do you offer any type of warranty on repairs?

Warranty depends on the repair category and the parts used. Clear warranty terms can be shared once the estimate is issued.

Do you offer pickup and delivery?

Yes, pickup and delivery can be arranged depending on your location and the nature of the device or installation request.

Can I pay through M-Pesa?

Yes. We support M-Pesa payments for deposits, balances, and full payment depending on the service.
